MARK ZUCKERBERG

Founder of Facebook

About

American business magnate, computer programmer, internet entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He co-founded the social media website Facebook and its parent company Meta Platforms, of which he is executive chairman, chief executive officer, and controlling shareholder.Zuckerberg attended Harvard University, where he launched Facebook in February 2004 with his roommates Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes. Originally launched in only select college campuses, the site expanded rapidly and eventually beyond colleges, reaching one billion users in 2012. Zuckerberg took the company public in May 2012 with majority shares. In 2007, at age 23, he became the world's youngest self-made billionaire.

About Meta

Meta is a social media platform and technology company founded by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates Andrew McCollum, Eduardo Saverin, Chris Hughes, and Dustin Moskovitz. It was launched on February 4, 2004, initially as a social networking website for Harvard University students and later expanded to other colleges and universities. Over the years, Facebook has grown into one of the largest and most influential social media platforms in the world.Facebook has played a significant role in shaping how people communicate, share information, and connect online. It has also been a platform for various social and political movements, making it a subject of both praise and controversy. As of my last knowledge update in September 2021, Facebook was undergoing significant changes and facing ongoing discussions regarding its impact on society and regulation. Please note that the platform and its features may have evolved since then.

Projects

Facebook

Facebook is a social media platform and technology company that was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg, along with his college roommates Andrew McCollum, Eduardo Saverin, Chris Hughes, and Dustin Moskovitz. Since its inception, Facebook has evolved significantly and played a pivotal role in shaping the landscape of social networking and online communication.

Instagram

Instagram is a popular social media platform that focuses on visual content sharing. It was created by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger and launched in October 2010. In 2012, Facebook acquired Instagram, making it one of the largest and most widely used social media platforms in the world.

Threads

"Threads" referred to a social media app developed by Instagram, which is owned by Facebook (now Meta Platforms, Inc.). Threads was designed as a companion app to Instagram and focused on private messaging and sharing with a user's closest friends or a select group of contacts.

Whatsapp

WhatsApp is a popular messaging application and platform known for its ease of use, end-to-end encryption, and widespread global adoption. WhatsApp is known for its strong commitment to user privacy and security. It employs end-to-end encryption, which means that only the sender and recipient of a message can read its contents, and not even WhatsApp itself has access to the message content.
